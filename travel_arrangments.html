Travel Arrangements

Booking & Confirmation

You can make a reservation online on either our website of mobile app. Should you wish to book a room orally, please reach us via our telephone or mobile number listed on the Contact Info page.

The details you'll need to provide while booking include:

  • Name
  • Address
  • Passport No.
  • Date of Arrival
  • Expected Date of Departure

Once you have made your reservation, you will receive a booking confirmation.

Transport To & From the Resort

For guests arriving and leaving via the airport, transport will be provided to and from the Resort for no additional charge, please note you may have to share the same vehicle with other guests.

Other transport options inlude taking the Lamu Ferry to the Resort's island or self-sailing to the Resort's dock on the island.
